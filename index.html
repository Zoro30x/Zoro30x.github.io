    Our Story

    1991

    Alain Morcel and Frederic Loeven created LM in 1991 and started their business in a 1200 sq. ft.
    shop on Masson Street in Montreal. Quickly recognized for their ability to produce high-end
    custom furniture, they quickly carved out a reputation for very high quality craftmanship in
    the field of cabinetmaking in Montreal.

    OUR SERVICES

    The customer experience is at the heart of our motivation. Although we work
    mainly with designers, architects and other professionals of the industry, we
    regularly work directly with the client for specific projects. We support you
    throughout your project.

    No. 1

    Measurements

    We will go to the site to take an accurate measurements necessary for the production of shop drawings.

    No. 2

    Workshop drawings

    We produce workshop drawings and 3D modeling. This step allows us to ensure that our understanding of the project matches the client's expectations. These drawings are also the link to the manufacturing.

    No. 3

    Manufacturing

    We specialize in the manufacturing of high-end custom-made residential, commercial and institutional furniture. Custom kitchens, bathroom furniture, walk-in closets and custom storages.

    No. 4

    Finish

    We proudly to to say that the finishing of our furniture is done on site at our workshops in our two finishing rooms by our trained painter teams.

    No. 5

    Installation

    Since a project is only finished once it is rooted in its environment, we offer installation services by our qualified workforce in the Montreal region and its surroundings for a perfect integration into your space.
